Multimodal LLM-Based Interaction System for Home Robots with Real-Time Adaptation

URIS approved projects

I lead this project under Prof. Wei Lou, funded by URIS, PolyU's university-wide undergraduate research scheme. It fuses voice, gesture, and visual context into one grounded dialogue loop for a robot operating in a home environment.

For the perception layer I fine-tuned Qwen2.5-VL-7B with DoRA on RefCOCO grounding (6× Ascend 910B2, 8.1 hours), raising acc@0.5 from 78.8% to 91.2% to give the system a reliable referring-expression backbone.

Auditing Perch V2 in BirdCLEF+ 2026: An eBird Coverage Limit and No Detectable Hard-Selection Headroom

Paper PDF Audit harness

Sole-author working note auditing the Perch V2 pipeline behind my Kaggle Bronze entry on the Brazilian Pantanal bioacoustic benchmark. It quantifies a 31-class eBird output coverage gap, and its bootstrap, permutation, and TOST tests show no reliable headroom from hard per-class selection.

The same analysis shows focal-to-soundscape domain shift dominates the small in-domain ensemble effects.

SurveyLens: A Discipline-Aware Benchmark for Automatic Survey Generation

I built SurveyLens-1k: 1,000 human-written surveys, 100 per discipline across 10 fields, curated from Semantic Scholar via keyword retrieval, LLM-based discipline tagging, and citation-rank pruning.

I also designed its three-stage cleaning pipeline (MinerU PDF parsing, rule filtering, LLM-assisted citation normalization), which raised valid-sample retention from 62% to 94%.

PersonalPlan: Planning Multi-Agent Systems for Personalized Programming Learning

I built the plan-to-CrewAI runtime mapper and the four-axis evaluation harness (instruction fidelity, workflow completeness, interaction quality, content correctness) that gates plans into the 3,043-instance MAP-PPL training set, running 100-plan batches in a multi-language Docker sandbox.

I then validated GRPO Phase 1 reward training on Qwen3-8B with LoRA: tier-1 mean reward rose from 0.41 to 0.90 in 156 steps. Along the way I traced the Qwen3 /no_think completion-truncation failure mode and wrote up a repro.
